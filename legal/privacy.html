Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jubly Reader collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the app, website, account features, reading tools, billing features, support tools, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, information created by your use of the Service, and information needed to operate account, billing, reading, and support features.

Account information

  • email address, username, display name, authentication identifiers, account status, and related profile information;
  • login, verification, password-reset, and session information handled through our authentication provider.

Reading and library information

  • books, documents, pasted text, uploaded files, converted text, metadata, library records, and import status;
  • reading progress, current place, page/chapter position, compact metrics, daily stats, and usage history;
  • settings and preferences needed to provide the reading experience.

Notes, flashcards, annotations, and user-created study content

If you use annotation tools, we may store notes, flashcard fronts/backs, selected text, page or block anchors, timestamps, and related metadata needed to show that content again in the right context.

Audio, TTS, AI, and processing information

When you use audio, text-to-speech, summary, anchor, evaluation, or similar features, we may process the relevant text, page segments, prompts, provider responses, timing data, generated audio references, diagnostics, and related metadata needed to provide and troubleshoot the feature.

Billing and plan information

We may store plan level, subscription status, trial status, entitlement snapshots, usage allowances, token or usage records, checkout status, billing portal status, and payment-related identifiers. Payment card details are processed by our payment processor and are not intended to be stored directly by Jubly Reader.

Technical and device information

We may collect browser type, device information, IP address, approximate location derived from network information, log data, error reports, diagnostics, performance information, cookies, local storage, session storage, cache data, and similar technical information.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • create, authenticate, secure, and maintain accounts;
  • provide reading, library, import, progress, annotation, notes, flashcards, audio, TTS, and AI-assisted features;
  • process subscriptions, trials, payments, usage allowances, tokens, billing portal actions, and entitlements;
  • sync durable user content and restore your place across sessions where supported;
  • respond to support requests, bug reports, suggestions, and safety or abuse reports;
  • debug, secure, monitor, improve, and protect the Service;
  • prevent fraud, abuse, scraping, security incidents, and unauthorized access;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Cookies, Local Storage, and Device Storage

Jubly Reader may use cookies, local storage, session storage, IndexedDB, browser cache, and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember local settings, preserve reading continuity, cache allowed content, support offline or fast local behavior where implemented, and reduce repeated processing.

Some heavy device-local assets, such as locally uploaded music or browser caches, may remain on your device unless you clear them through the app or browser controls.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers and processors who help us operate the Service, including providers for:

  • hosting and deployment;
  • authentication, database, storage, and serverless functions;
  • payment processing, subscriptions, invoices, fraud prevention, and billing portal services;
  • email, support, diagnostics, error monitoring, security, and abuse prevention;
  • AI, text-to-speech, audio generation, conversion, or related processing features.

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or a valid government request; to protect rights, safety, security, and property; to enforce our Terms; or in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or sale of assets.

5. Third-Party Services

Jubly Reader may rely on third-party services such as Supabase for authentication, database, and storage-related infrastructure; Stripe for payment and subscription processing; Microsoft Azure or other providers for text-to-speech or AI-related processing; Vercel or other hosting providers for deployment; and email/support providers for user communications.

These providers process information according to their own terms, privacy policies, security practices, and data-processing agreements. Provider availability, policies, and regions may change over time.

6. Imported Content and Sensitive Information

You control what you import, paste, upload, annotate, or process. Please avoid uploading highly sensitive information unless you are comfortable with it being processed by Jubly Reader and its service providers as needed to provide the requested features.

Jubly Reader is not designed for regulated medical, legal, financial, government-classified, or other highly sensitive records unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service, maintain your account, preserve reading continuity, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and enforce agreements.

Some account-backed content may remain until you delete it or request deletion, while certain logs, billing records, security records, backups, and legally required records may be retained for longer periods.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No internet service, browser storage system, or transmission method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices and Requests

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, or restrict certain personal information. You can make privacy or account-data requests through the in-app support or feedback surface provided by Jubly Reader, or through the support contact published by the deployed app.

You may also control some local browser data by clearing cookies, local storage, IndexedDB, caches, or site data through your browser. Clearing local data may remove local-only settings, cached content, or device-local assets.

10. Children

Jubly Reader is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 provided personal information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

11. International Use

If you use Jubly Reader from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where Jubly Reader or its service providers operate. Those countries may have different data-protection laws than your location.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Service after updates means the updated policy applies.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the in-app support or feedback surface provided by Jubly Reader, or the support contact published by the deployed app.
