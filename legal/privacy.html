Jubly Reader

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jubly Reader collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

1. Information we collect

Depending on how you use Jubly Reader, we may collect:

  • Account information: email address, username, authentication identifiers, account status, and profile details.
  • Library and reading information: imported titles, document metadata, reading progress, page/chapter position, reading history, preferences, notes, flashcards, annotations, and related anchors.
  • Content you provide: uploaded files, pasted text, imported reading materials, highlighted text, and annotation content.
  • Usage and plan information: subscription status, plan tier, token or usage records, import-slot usage, feature access, and billing-related identifiers.
  • Technical information: device, browser, app diagnostics, local storage state, cookies or similar technologies, IP-derived security data, logs, and error information.

2. How we use information

We use information to operate, secure, and improve Jubly Reader, including to create accounts, restore libraries and reading progress, sync account-backed data, process imports, generate text-to-speech audio, provide notes and flashcards, enforce limits, manage subscriptions, prevent abuse, troubleshoot issues, and communicate important service information.

3. Uploaded and imported content

Imported files, pasted text, annotations, notes, flashcards, and reading metadata may be stored or processed so the app can provide library, reading, search, navigation, TTS, sync, and revisit features. Do not upload content you are not allowed to use with the service.

4. Text-to-speech, AI, and processors

To provide features, Jubly Reader may send relevant text, metadata, or requests to third-party processors such as hosting providers, Supabase for authentication/database services, Stripe for payments, cloud storage providers, and TTS or AI providers. These processors may handle information only as needed to provide their services to Jubly Reader.

5. Payments

Payments and subscription management may be handled by Stripe or another payment processor. Jubly Reader does not need to store full payment card numbers. We may receive billing status, customer identifiers, plan information, subscription status, invoice state, and payment-result information needed to operate paid features.

6. Cookies, local storage, and device storage

The app may use cookies, browser local storage, IndexedDB, or similar device storage for authentication, session continuity, local reading state, appearance preferences, imported content cache, diagnostics, and app functionality. Some local data may stay on your device unless synced or removed through app actions.

7. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate Jubly Reader, when required by law, to protect rights and safety, to prevent abuse or fraud, or as part of a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset sale.

8. Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to operate the service, maintain your account and library, comply with legal or billing obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent abuse, and support backups or security logs. Deleted content may persist for a limited time in backups, logs, or records where required for legitimate operational or legal reasons.

9. Your choices

You may be able to update account details, delete library items, remove notes or flashcards, manage billing, clear local browser data, or request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy assistance through the support or feedback tools provided in the app. Some requests may require identity verification.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No online service can guarantee perfect security, and you are responsible for keeping your account credentials safe.

11. Children

Jubly Reader is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us through the support or feedback tools in the app so we can review and respond.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date shows when the current version took effect. Continued use of the service after updates means the updated policy applies.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the support or feedback tools provided in the app.
