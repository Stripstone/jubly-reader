📘 How This Works

Your goal is simple.

Read the passage carefully. Write a short consolidation of what the passage actually does. Then choose how to evaluate it: see which core anchors you captured, or get AI feedback and a clarity score.

"If someone only read your consolidation, would they understand how the passage works?"

💡 What Makes a Strong Consolidation?

  • Captures the core idea
  • Finished within the character limit
  • Discovers keywords and anchors in the text
